Introduction to Web Hosting

Web hosting is a service that allows individuals and organizations to make their websites accessible via the internet. When you create a website, the files and data that make up your site need to be stored somewhere. Web hosting providers offer the technologies and services needed to store these files on their servers and ensure your website is available to users online.

Important of Hosting a Web site Online

Hosting your website online makes it accessible to anyone with an internet connection, expanding your reach globally. This is essential for businesses, organizations, and individuals who want to connect with a broader audience, increase visibility, and engage with potential customers or followers from different parts of the world.
